My Work

Shopping - Flower Shop Mobile App UI/UX Design Process

Skills: Sketch, Craft, Unsplash, InVision, Interviewing

I worked in a flower shop before and wanted to create a floral shopping app. I knew a few companies that provided bouquets and such. Researched on what colors to provide for my app by asking friends and searching to find common flower colors.

  • “Your one exotic flower shop!” - Blossom is a shopping mobile app, in providing bouquets and unique flowers from around the world

My goal as a UI and UX designer, was to design an app for users, who are looking for exotic flower from around the world. I created user flows and diagrams.

Who is the audience?

For anyone, who are looking for a floral gift on occassions and or plant enthusiasts.

User flows

New User: Finding a unique bouquet for special occassions

Welcome Page > Login Page > New User? > Create Account > Home Page

Returning User: As a returning user, I want to checkout their latest floral designs

Welcome Page > Login Page > Home Page

Low and mid fidelity wireframes

Created a low fidelity wireframes after finishing the user flow diagram.

Designed the Mid fidelity wireframes, after completing low wireframes for user testing.

User Feedback after User Testing:

“Straight forward app and it is intuitive”

“Clean and elegant design relating to it’s floral app theme”

“I like how you use text instead of icons for your navigation bar.”

“The ‘filter and sort’ drop down buttons, were confusing to use”

User Test Analysis:

Pain Point

Adding more variety under “filters”

Replacing the fill-in boxes, with circles instead

Removing the burger menu

Placing the navigation bar, onto the bottom of the app

Recommendations

Could add another category, such as origin of the flower.

Considering changing them into circles, to make it look inviting and friendly to the user.

It may give simple options to the user, but the app offers a variety of categories.

Keeping the navigation bar. Going to remove the “Occassion” and replacing it with “Contact us”

Moodboard:

Final

Purpose: “Blossom” is a shop mobile app created for users who wants to shop exotic flowers

Objective: The objective is providing bouquets and unique flowers from around the world

My Role: UX and UI Designer

Duration: May 2020 - June 2020

Steps:

  • Decided on a flower shop mobile app
  • Researched similar apps such as Florists' Transworld Delivery and 1-800-Flowers
  • Designed mood board
  • Created user flow for both new and returning users
  • Designed Low Fidelity wireframes
  • Researched apps through iOS and Android mobile platforms
  • Designed Mid Fidelity wireframes
  • Design final mockups

Conclusion:

What went well?

Using my passion for flowers and gardening and translating them onto a mobile app.

What didn’t go well?

In my mood board, I first chose blue and purple to represent the Blossom App, then changed to pink since some users relate its color to flowers. It was difficult to find a complementary color to pink.

What could be improved?

Adding in icons onto the burger menu.
